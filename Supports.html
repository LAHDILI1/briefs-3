In the beginning, Silicon Valley

To understand Schoolab’s DNA, we need to go back to its origins. In 2005, the 3 founders, with their experience as entrepreneurs and inspired by their visit to the the Silicon Valley in the United States, made 2 impactful observations:
> European companies were cultivating very strong internal silos, paralyzing their capacity for innovation. These silos originated in an educational system divided between training courses unable to dialogue with each other.
> On the other hand, innovative design in the United States offered an immense untapped potential for developing useful innovation which is adapted to user’s needs.

Helping companies maximize their positive impact within their ecosystem

We believe in the power of Design, Inclusion and Innovation to bring about new models. We create responsible and sustainable innovation by connecting our clients and their projects to our ecosystems and by revealing the entrepreneurial and collaborative qualities of each individual.

Frequently Asked Questions.

What types of startups do you work with?

We work with startups from various industries, including tech, healthcare, e-commerce, and more. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each startup.

How can your company help secure funding for my startup ?

We assist in connecting startups with potential investors and provide guidance on preparing pitches and securing investment. We also offer resources on different funding options available.

Do you offer co-working spaces for startups?

Yes, we provide co-working spaces equipped with essential facilities to foster collaboration and networking among startups.

What sets your consulting services apart from others?

Our consultants have hands-on experience in the startup world. They provide personalized guidance on strategy, business development, and more. We prioritize your success and offer ongoing support.

How can I apply for your startup support program?

To apply for our program, please visit our website and fill out the application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you for further steps.

What success stories can you share from startups you've supported?

We've had the privilege of supporting numerous successful startups. You can find some of their stories and testimonials on our website to see the impact of our services.
